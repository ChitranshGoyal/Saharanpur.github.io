Saharanpur

It is situated in the doab region between Yamuna and Ganga. Hindon river originates in Saharanpur and flows through Merrut, Modinagar, Gaziabad. (Hindon air base in Ghaziabad)

Saharanpur borders Himachal Pradesh, Haryana & Uttarakhand. So you can easily visit Ambala, Dehradun, Roorkee, Haridwar, Chandigarh, Delhi easily from here.

It also has a campus of IIT Roorkee dedicated to Paper and Pulp Engineering among other courses. Star paper mills is also there in Saharanpur.

Saharanpur is the backbone of the trade which flows into Uttarakhand

It is a middle tier smart city with comfortable life, a multi screen cinema hall and decent earnings opportunities.
